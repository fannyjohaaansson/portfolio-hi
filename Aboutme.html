About me.

Hi! My name is Fanny Johansson and I am a graphic designer from Sweden. I’m currently studying front end developer at hyper island in Stockholm. I love to photograph and edit pictures on my free time.

I am born and raised in Västerås and lived there until I finished high school. Right after high school I moved up north to Sundsvall to study Graphic design and communication. I got my bachelors degree this June and at the end of the summer I moved to Stockholm so I could study frontend developer at hyper island. Which also is what my occupation is at the moment.
